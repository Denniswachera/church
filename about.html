Our branch in Samburu where we distributed footstuff to the people of God. This mission is part of our vision of reaching those who have never been reached by the gospel, especially those who are in remote parts of the country.


History

The church was founded in 1997, Nakuru. This is where our motherchurch is located. The earliest branch was at Kaptembwo where we started with a few church members, currently we have expended to most major towns.

Leadership

The leadership of the church is Jesus christ through his servant Apostle Johanna. Motherchurch leadership is Pastor Ochieng.


Branches

We have several branches within Kenya and Ethipiopia.

Some of these branches include:

  • Kabarnet
  • Kericho
  • Bahati
  • Kaptembwo
  • Nairobi
  • Gilgil

and many more!
You are welcome to visit and fellowship in any of our branches. Be blessed.
